Our Story

Traditional baking since 2020

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in a small home kitchen by Katlego Chose, Delightful Bakery began with just one oven and a dream to bring authentic artisan baking to our community.

Today, we still use Anna's original sourdough starter (nicknamed "Doughlene") in all our breads, maintaining the same commitment to quality that won us our first loyal customers.

Our Baking Philosophy

Quality Ingredients

We source organic flour, free-range eggs, and European butter for every recipe

Traditional Timing

Our sourdough ferments for 24+ hours to develop perfect flavor

Made with Love

Every item is handcrafted by our passionate baking team
